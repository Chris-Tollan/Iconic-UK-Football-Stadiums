Hampden Park
  • Hampden Park
  • Home of The Scottish National Football Team
  • 51866 Capacity
  • Built in 1903 and renovated in 1999
  • Visit hampdenpark.co.uk

Located in Glasgow, Scotland, Hampden Park is one of the

most Iconic Stadiums in World Football.
Aside from hosting the Scottish National Teams home
fixtures the stadium also host annual domestic club
Scottish Cup and League Cup finals.
Hampden has also been used for some of the the biggest
matches in the world including European Cup Finals
as well as hosting International Cup compeitions.
It is also regularly used to hold other sporting events
and concerts.
The stadium features onsite restaurants and bars and
also offers guided tours of the museum and stadium.

Address

Hampden Park, Letherby Drive, Glasgow, G42 9BA

Celtic Park
  • Celtic Park
  • Home of Celtic Football CLub
  • Capacity - 60411
  • 1892 and then renovated from 1994-1998
  • Visit celticfc.com

Celtic Park, also known as Parkhead and

more commonly to the clubs supporters as
Paradise is the largest stadium in Scotland.
Celtic have one of the most passionate fanbases
in world football with the atmosphere often voted
as the best in Europe!
To feel Europes best atmosphere in its full capacity,
attending a |Celtic v Rangers match here is a must
This game, known as the "old firm" is globally recognised
as one of, if not the biggest derby in world football.
Stadium tours are availble where you will be able to
see Celtic European Cup from their famous 1967 win!

Address

Celtic Park, The Celtic Way, Parkhead, Glasgow, G40 3RE
