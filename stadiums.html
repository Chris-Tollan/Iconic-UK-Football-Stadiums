Hampden Park
  • Hampden Park
  • Home of The Scottish National Football Team
  • Capacity - 51866
  • Opened - 1903 and renovated in 1999
  • Visit hampdenpark.co.uk

Located in Glasgow, Scotland, Hampden Park is one of the most Iconic Stadiums in World Football. Aside from hosting the Scottish National Teams home fixtures the stadium also hosts annual domestic club Scottish Cup and League Cup finals. Hampden has also been used for some of the the biggest matches in the world including European Cup Finals as well as hosting International Cup compeitions. It is also regularly used to hold other sporting events and concerts. The stadium features onsite restaurants and bars and also offers guided tours of the museum and stadium.

Address

Hampden Park, Letherby Drive, Glasgow, G42 9BA

Celtic Park
  • Celtic Park
  • Home of Celtic Football CLub
  • Capacity - 60411
  • Opened - 1892 and then renovated from 1994-1998
  • Visit celticfc.com

Celtic Park, also known as Parkhead and more commonly to the clubs supporters as Paradise is the largest stadium in Scotland. Celtic have one of the most passionate fanbases in world football with the atmosphere often voted as the best in Europe! To feel Europes best atmosphere in its full capacity, attending a |Celtic v Rangers match here is a must This game, known as the "old firm" is globally recognised as one of, if not the biggest derby in world football. Stadium tours are availble where you will be able to see Celtic European Cup from their famous 1967 win!

Address

Celtic Park, The Celtic Way, Parkhead, Glasgow, G40 3RE

Ibrox
  • Ibrox
  • Home to Rangers Football Club
  • Capacity - 50817
  • Opened - 1899 and renovated multiple times
  • Visit rangers.co.uk

Located in the heart of Govan in Glasgow Ibrox is everything a football stadium should be. Every inch of the stadium tells the history of the football club and just how much it means to the supporters. a Rangers v Celtic "old firm" match here should be on every football fans bucket list! The stadium offers tours, a merchandise superstore, several restaurants and lounges and a matchday experience to rival and sport, anywhere in the world.

Address

Ibrox, Edminston Drive, Govan, Glasgow, G51 2XD

Wembley
  • Wembley
  • Home of the England National Team
  • Capacity - 90000
  • Opened 2007
  • Visit wembleystadium.com

Perhaps the most famous sporting stadium in the world. Often referred to as the home of football Wembley is the Icon of Iconic UK stadiums Built on the site of the originally Wembley Stadium the current one maintains many similarities of the old versions that once stood here with a modern twist Used frequently for English, European and Internationl cup finals there is plenty of oppertunity to attend fixtures here. The stadium annually hosts NFL games and is also a regular venue for some of the biggest boxing nights and concerts in the world. The stadium offers tours, shopping, hospitality and so much more. With it being nestled in the centre of London the stadium is easily accessable meaning there is no reason not to visit!

Address

Wembley Stadium, Olympic Way, London, HA9 0WS

Anfield
  • Anfield
  • Home of Liverpool Football Club
  • Capacity - 53395

  • Opened 1884, the stadium has undergone a number of renovations thorughout the years and is currently being redeveloped whilst remaining fully active.

  • Visit liverpoolfc.com

Anfield is home to one of the most successfull football clubs on the planet! The stadium oozes class and is located right in the heart of the local community. The second you arrive at Anfield you know you are somewhere special, with the mureals painted on the surrounding houses and statues surrounding the ground all dedicated to club heros from the past and present. The Spion Kop is one of the most notorious stadium stands in the world and is recognised for the influence the fans who sit there can have on matches purely by the atmosphere they create. Anfield offers tours and a matchday experience that will make the hairs on the back of your neck stand up!

Address

Anfield, Anfield Road, Liverpool, L4 0TH

Old Trafford
  • Old Trafford
  • Home of Manchester United Football Club
  • Capacity - 74310

  • Opened 1910, the stadium has since had a number of renovations, most recently in 2006

  • Visit manutd.com

Nicknamed the Theatre of Dreams, Old Trafford is a stadium that needs no introduction The biggest names in world football have often been heard say it was a dream to play here. The stadium offers everything a fan could dream of, from museums and stadium tours to some of the biggest fixtures in football. Manchester United are a footballing powerhouse and its easy to see why from the second you arrive at Old Trafford.

Address

old Trafford, Sir Matt Busby Way, Manchester, M16 0RA
